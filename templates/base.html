About this app

Track how far you drove, your vehicle’s miles per gallon, and the price you paid at the pump. The app estimates gallons used, total fuel cost, and cost per mile, then keeps a simple history so you can compare trips over time.

Built with Flask, SQLite, and Jinja — portfolio-ready and easy to run locally or in Docker Desktop.
